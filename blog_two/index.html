My Journey to the college.

July, 28

Everything started with the result of Intermediate. I secured 70% in Intermediate and decided to go for year gap for the preparation of JEE to get into IITs with a Computer Science Engineering. But, god did'nt want me to go for a gap of whole one year. I have given entrance for Engineering and got a private admission in private Institution which is only 15 km from my current location or we can say from home. It was the first I entered the college. Did'nt know more about the college and there corridor. On First day, I was typical be having like student of HighSchool with socially connected to any individual. Roaming here and there, exploring the college area, canteen (for the name only ), ground and all. Attending lectures like a school boy. Later On Many More Student were coming day by day in the college, and having a interaction with is like I know them for a long period of time. Make too many friends in the class. Let me tell you the curriculum of the first year of engineering in the next section.

First Year

First Year Started, with a common subject for all which we have in my sense studied earlier in the HighSchool and Intermediate. With a few new subjects i.e Basic Electrical Engineering, Introduction to Engineering Mechanics, Workshop (Majdoor banane ki kala) etc. But, Having fun with these subject is next thing. I have a question in my mind why these subject is common to all branches in the first year. I got the answer in the few day that taking an example when we go in sweett shop for buy stuff, we had to taste which sweet is good to buy, similarly, like that having taste of every branch of engineering in the first year will decide which branch like most by there subjects. Like Choosing Electrical Engineering after studying Electrical book , Mechanical Engineering by Mechanics , Computer Science Engineering with a programming in the first year.


Studied One Year and given Exam and Chooed Computer Science Engineering for next three years. Examination process has its own story we discuss it later for now good bye and take care.
